About

Bio

I’m a product leader who moves comfortably between vision and execution — someone who can frame a problem for the business, align executives around a direction, and still stay grounded in what users actually need. My foundation is a Finance degree from McGill University, followed by early-career roles in Finance & Operations at technology startups and scaleups. That background shaped how I think about tradeoffs, metrics, and sustainable growth.

I’ve built a track record of shipping successful 0-to-1 products by combining rigorous discovery with empathy for the people on the other side of the experience. I’m as comfortable speaking the language of the business and executive team as I am working with design and engineering to get something real into the market.
