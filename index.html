Kaelan Forgues

Product leader. Complexity welcome.

I started my career in Finance and Operations at tech startups and scale-ups, and took the long way into Product. That detour was the best thing that happened to my career. Working inside fast-growing companies meant spending a lot of time with CEOs and senior leadership early on, and that shaped how I communicate product strategy: I know how to frame a problem in terms executives care about, and how to build the case for something new in a way that optimizes for both user outcomes and initiative buy-in.

I’ve shipped 0-to-1 products across two-sided marketplaces, AI, and consumer platforms. I lead with data and UXR, and don’t advocate for a direction until I understand the problem well enough to defend the solution. I care about execution as much as strategy. The best product thinking doesn’t count if it doesn’t ship, and the best product ideas don’t count if users don’t love them.

What keeps me interested in building is that the surface keeps changing. I’ve gone from native apps to loyalty platforms to conversational AI, and I’m still figuring it out as I go. I’m most comfortable in the unknown. I like grey area, new thinking, and trying something that hasn’t been done before.
